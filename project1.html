About

HR-Trennungsmanager helps to manage employee separation process smoothly as possible. It can be used as a comprehensive guideline for stressless separating employees. A content of the App includes information, resources, links and three calculators, which help to evaluate all costs of employee separation to complete this important process. This app is created for people, who work in a field of employment law and HR practitioners.

User Persona

Thomas Age 27 Occupation: Employment law consultant Location: Stuttgart

Behaviors:

Representing clients’ interests online and communicating via email, telephone and face-to-face; Visiting client companies and dealing with specific issues; Creating documents of support relating to employment law using various programs and systems, i.e. office word, PowerPoint, producing employment contract

Needs and goals:

Make the procedure simpler and less painful; Getting a quick overview and assessment of situation of the employee process separation; Drafting all details of employee separation without specifically preparing for it.

User Centered Design Canvas
