About

Belic-Ya is a small travel agency based in Ulan-Ude. It currently offers tours and excursions, activities and tickets around Buryatia Republic. The agency needed a landing page for: attracting more international tourists; informing customers about their service; creating user touch-points; increasing conversion.

Customer Journey

Travel decision-making process consist of five stages: dreaming, planning, booking, experiencing and sharing. „Dreaming" that happens when people are exploring destination options and ideas with no firm plans. At this stage, people are looking for inspiration. „Planning" that happens when people have chosen a destination. They're looking for the right dates, the right flight, the right place to stay, and all the things they'll do while they're there. „Booking" that happens when the research is done, and people are ready to book their tickets. "Experiencing" that happens when the trip is underway. "Sharing" that happens when the trip is underway or over. Travelers share their experience with others on social media about the trip they've been dreaming about.

Colors

Layout

There are 12 columns (8 columns, 2 columns: based on breakpoints) in the grid system. Column widths change with the size of the grid. Gutter widths are fixed values (32 px, 16px, etc.) based on breakpoints. Grid margins have fixed width of 128 px. Layout regions can span any number of columns and are separated by the grid gutter.

Iconography

Icons are universal and simple. Each icon communicates the core idea of a service.
